# School SaaS Role System

## 1. Platform (SaaS Level Roles)

- **SUPER_ADMIN**
  - Owns the SaaS platform
  - Manages all schools, billing, system configuration

- **SUPPORT_ADMIN**
  - Handles user support
  - Can view logs but not modify core financial data

- **SYSTEM_AUDITOR**
  - Read-only access to audit logs and system activity

---

## 2. School Administration Roles (Tenant-Specific)

- **SCHOOL_OWNER / PROPRIETOR**
  - Ultimate authority within a school

- **HEADMASTER / PRINCIPAL**
  - Operational control of entire school

- **VICE PRINCIPAL**
  - Assists principal, manages discipline and operations

- **SCHOOL_ADMIN**
  - System administrator inside school software

- **ACADEMIC_COORDINATOR**
  - Manages curriculum, subjects, exams

- **ADMIN_OFFICER**
  - Handles records, documentation, enrollment

---

## 3. Teaching & Academic Roles

- **TEACHER**
  - Standard teaching staff

- **HEAD_OF_DEPARTMENT (HOD)**
  - Supervises subject department

- **CLASS_TEACHER**
  - Responsible for a specific class

- **EXAM_OFFICER**
  - Manages exams, grading, and results processing

- **LAB_INSTRUCTOR**
  - Practical/lab-based teaching (science, ICT)

- **SUBSTITUTE_TEACHER**
  - Temporary teaching staff

---

## 4. Student & Guardian Roles

- **STUDENT**
  - Learner entity

- **PARENT / GUARDIAN**
  - Monitors student performance, attendance, and fees

---

## 5. Support / Operational Staff Roles

- **ACCOUNTANT / FINANCE_OFFICER**
  - Handles fees, payments, and financial reports

- **RECEPTIONIST**
  - Front desk operations and admissions

- **LIBRARIAN**
  - Library management

- **IT_ADMIN**
  - System technical support inside school
